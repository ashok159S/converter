What Is a PDF Rotator?

A PDF Rotator is an online tool that changes the orientation of PDF pages by rotating them 90°, 180°, or 270°. It is perfect for fixing scanned documents, correcting sideways pages, and improving document readability before sharing or printing.

Why Rotate PDF Pages?

  • Fix incorrectly scanned documents.
  • Correct sideways or upside-down pages.
  • Improve readability on all devices.
  • Prepare documents for printing.
  • Create professional-looking PDFs.
