What Is a PDF Watermark Tool?

A PDF Watermark tool allows you to add text or image watermarks to PDF documents. Watermarks help protect intellectual property, indicate document status, strengthen branding, and discourage unauthorized copying or distribution.

Why Add Watermarks to PDFs?

  • Protect copyrighted documents.
  • Add company logos or branding.
  • Mark files as "Confidential," "Draft," or "Approved."
  • Discourage unauthorized copying.
  • Create professional business documents.
