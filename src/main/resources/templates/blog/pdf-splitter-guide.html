What Is a PDF Splitter?

A PDF Splitter is an online tool that allows you to divide a large PDF document into smaller files. You can extract specific pages or split an entire document into multiple PDFs without affecting the original quality.

Why Split PDF Files?

  • Extract only the pages you need.
  • Reduce the size of large PDF documents.
  • Share selected pages instead of the entire file.
  • Organize long documents into smaller sections.
  • Improve document management.
