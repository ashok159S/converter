What Is a PDF Crop Tool?

A PDF Crop tool allows you to remove unwanted margins, white space, or unnecessary areas from PDF pages. Cropping helps improve document appearance, focus on important content, and prepare files for printing or digital sharing.

Why Crop PDF Pages?

  • Remove unnecessary white margins.
  • Focus attention on important content.
  • Prepare documents for professional printing.
  • Improve the appearance of scanned PDFs.
  • Create cleaner and more readable documents.
