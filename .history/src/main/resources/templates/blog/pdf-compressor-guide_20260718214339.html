What Is a PDF Compressor?

A PDF Compressor is an online tool that reduces the file size of PDF documents while maintaining good quality. Smaller PDF files are easier to upload, share through email, store on devices, and download faster.

Why Compress PDF Files?

  • Reduce large PDF file sizes.
  • Send PDFs easily through email.
  • Meet website upload size limits.
  • Save storage space on your device.
  • Speed up file sharing and downloads.
